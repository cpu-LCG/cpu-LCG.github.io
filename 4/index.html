Welcome to Hao Lab

Our research focuses on...
For example: Our research focuses on...developing and implementing an array of novel analytical tools in the area of metabolomics and chemical proteomics to elucidate the direct binding targets of small molecules including natural medicines and endogenous metabolites, and gain new insights into how these functional molecules regulate protein function and signaling network.
Hao Lab is led by Prof. Haiping Hao, the Yangtse River Scholar and the recipient of the National Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars. His current group includes over 10 co-PIs, 5 postdoctoral fellows and 100 graduate students. The super-lab is equipped with state-of-the-art mass spectrometer facilities for target discovery and molecular biology tools for target validation.
